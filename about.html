Who is Philippe R.J. Augustin?

Roger Jean Philippe Augustin is a civil engineer graduated from the State University of Haiti and pursued advanced studies in structural engineering at the City College of New York. He has extensive experience as a professor, engineer, and IT consultant. His skills include web development, database management, geospatial information, project management, audio production, and video editing.

He has held various significant positions, including professor at Juvenat Des Frères de l'Instruction Chrétienne and Institution Saint-Louis de Gonzague, engineer attached to the Office of the Minister of Public Works, IT Service Director at ONA, and office automation manager at UNIBANK S.A. He also co-founded the musical group STRINGS and participated in educational music composition projects.

As the Director of the Electoral Register and head of the IT Unit at CEP, he supervised critical electoral operations, including managing electoral lists and coordinating electoral commissions. He was a database consultant for UNOPS and played a key role in various international seminars and meetings on electoral processes.
